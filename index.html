Operational Challenge

The opportunity was bigger than building an app.

Event information, service requests, approvals, documents, and reporting were handled through separate files and repeated communication. The goal was to create one structured solution that supported both daily operations and management visibility.

01

Scattered event information

Operational information was distributed across multiple files and communication channels.

02

Manual follow-up

Request progress depended heavily on repeated emails and individual follow-up.

03

Limited workflow visibility

Users could not easily see the current stage, responsible person, timing, or completed actions.

04

Disconnected reporting

Operational activity and management reporting were not presented through one connected view.

How I Approach HR Challenges

From process understanding to a working digital solution.

My role was to identify the operational need, define the right structure, implement the solution, and ensure the dashboard answered the questions HR stakeholders actually needed.
